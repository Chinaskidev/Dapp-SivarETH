"Your support can transform lives, one medication and one tooth at a time."

In El Salvador, it is estimated that between 60,000 and 120,000 people suffer from epilepsy, and we face a significant challenge in obtaining essential anticonvulsant medications. Although the healthcare system provides coverage for several medications, availability is limited, especially at the basic levels of care.

Sivar Smile is forging a new path through community donations in cryptocurrencies, using blockchain technology with innovative funding sources. We primarily raise funds to obtain essential anticonvulsant medications for people with epilepsy and provide support to those who have suffered dental damage due to seizures. Our goal is to transform lives, ensuring strong and confident smiles with each contribution received.
