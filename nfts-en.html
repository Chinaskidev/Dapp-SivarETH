Support us and be part of the change by minting "Sivar-Regens." By doing so, you'll contribute to our mission of providing essential treatments and anticonvulsant medications to people with epilepsy. Your participation not only helps transform lives but also promotes innovation in funding charitable causes through Web3 technology. Join us and make a significant difference today.

Sivar-regens are an unlimited pixel art collection that you can mint for 10 USDC on the Base Network.

The funds will be used to purchase anticonvulsant medications. The artworks are inspired by $REGEN & $DEGEN, paying tribute to animals that have fallen victim to extinction and those currently endangered. We not only celebrate the beauty of nature but also actively contribute to its preservation. Each piece is a symbol of hope and action for a sustainable future.
