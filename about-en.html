About Us

The purpose of Sivar Smile is to provide assistance to people with epilepsy who face difficulties in obtaining essential medications and dental treatments. We focus on offering access to anticonvulsant medications, which are often inaccessible due to their high cost, and dental assistance to those who have lost teeth due to epileptic seizures, thereby improving their quality of life and well-being. At Sivar Smile, we leverage Web3 technology and the sale of exclusive NFTs to raise funds that go directly towards necessary treatments. Our commitment is to ensure that every contribution makes a significant impact, bringing hope and improving the quality of life for our beneficiaries. We work with transparency and dedication to transform lives, one medication and one tooth at a time.

• Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation for people with epilepsy in El Salvador.

• Mission

To finance dental treatments and provide essential medications through the sale of NFTs and the receipt of cryptocurrency donations, using the transparency and traceability of blockchain to ensure that every contribution effectively improves the lives of people with epilepsy.
